About Us

Founded in 2025, Bilkent Game Development & Design Club is a student-driven organization dedicated to fostering creativity and collaboration in the gaming industry. Our mission is to provide a platform where students can learn about game development, create their own projects, and engage with professionals in the industry. Through workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities, we aim to build a strong community of developers, designers, and storytellers.

Upcoming Events

We organize a variety of events throughout the academic year, including hands-on workshops, game jam competitions, and guest talks with industry experts. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, our events are designed to help you grow and refine your skills.

Our Projects

Our club members have worked on a variety of exciting game projects, from indie platformers to immersive RPGs. Each project is an opportunity to explore new ideas, experiment with game mechanics, and collaborate with fellow developers. We showcase our best work at game expos and competitions.

Contact Us

If you're interested in joining the club or collaborating with us, feel free to reach out. Follow us on social media, email us with inquiries, or join our Discord server for real-time discussions.
